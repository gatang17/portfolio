Juicy Splash

For the Juicy Splash project, I was tasked with designing the homepage to simulate the brand’s spirit. The goal was to visually capture the fresh, vibrant essence of a juice brand, focusing on creating an engaging first impression. This homepage design was created in Figma, with warm, energetic colors that evoke a summer vibe, reflecting the brand’s identity of refreshment and vitality.

In addition to the homepage design, I also created the brand’s full visual identity, including the logo, advertisements, and corporate stationery, as well as Point of Purchase (POP) materials. All of these were designed using Adobe Illustrator. The brand’s colors were chosen to reflect a bold, summery feel, using warm tones that convey energy, freshness, and vitality. The overall design communicates the dynamic nature of the brand and helps establish a clear visual identity.
