Annual Report

Advertising Design | Individual Project
Client: Communication Arts Technology Program (CATP)
School: Jefferson Community and Technical College, Louisville, KY
Expected Completion: 2024
Theme Colors: Red and White (based on official logo)

Project Objective

Design a visually compelling Annual Report that highlights the mission, accomplishments, and future goals of the Communication Arts Technology Program. The report aims to engage and inform a wide audience—taxpayers, administrators, students, and potential grant partners—while promoting continued support through scholarships and funding.

Creative Strategy

  • Use student work and photography to tell the story of CATP’s impact on the community and state
  • Showcase program awards, student achievements, and faculty excellence
  • Emphasize the uniqueness of CATP, including the only Commercial Photography track in the region
  • Incorporate interviews, portraits, and original layouts to reflect the identity of the program

Key Goals

  • Inform and inspire stakeholders
  • Reinforce the value of public arts and technology education
  • Reflect the professional quality and legacy of a 50-year-old program
  • Deliver an original, full-color, high-impact design rooted in strategy and storytelling

This project represents my ability to combine design, communication, and research into a unified visual narrative.

Advertising Design – Packaging Project

Client: Conceptual Brand / Re-design

Course: Advertising Design

Year: 2025

Objective

This individual project required creating an original or re-designed product identity for a physical item of significant size. The branding system had to include packaging, a point-of-purchase display, and a magazine ad.

Target Audience

  • Retail customers depending on product type
  • General consumers shopping in stores
  • Marketing teams and brand strategists

Deliverables

  • Product name and brand profile
  • Original logo design
  • Slogan and Unique Selling Proposition (USP)
  • Full packaging design with labels (mocked up in Adobe Dimension)
  • Magazine ad (8.5" x 11") featuring the product
  • 3D POP display concept and mockup
  • Photograph or layout showing all elements together

Skills Applied

  • Brand development and positioning
  • Packaging and label design
  • Creative advertising layout
  • Mockup rendering using Adobe Dimension
  • Conceptual storytelling and emotional appeal
  • Research and strategic thinking

Creative Approach

  • Started from product research and brainstorming
  • Explored several rough packaging concepts via sketches
  • Created bold, unique, and store-ready visuals
  • Used original photography, props, and layout for ad execution
  • Focused on a cohesive visual message across packaging and promotion
